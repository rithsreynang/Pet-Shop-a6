Our Story

Happy Pets, founded in 1950, began as a small family-owned pet shop in a bustling town. The visionary founder, Mr. Sokhom Hean, had a deep love for animals and a passion for promoting responsible pet ownership.

Our Mission

Our mission at Paws and Whiskers Pet Shop is to provide a loving and nurturing environment for pets, while offering exceptional products and services to pet owners. We are dedicated to promoting responsible pet ownership, educating the community about animal welfare.
