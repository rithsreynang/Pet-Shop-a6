Our Story

Happy Pets, founded in 1950, began as a small family-owned pet shop in a bustling town. The visionary founder, Mr. Sokhom Hean, had a deep love for animals and a passion for promoting responsible pet ownership.

Our Mission

The mission of Happy Pet Shop is to provide pet owners with a comprehensive range of high-quality pet products and services, while promoting the well-being and happiness of pets.
